# Choking

Choking is the performance collapse that occurs when _Self 1_ (the conscious mind) attempts to micromanage the execution of a stroke that _Self 2_ (the body) is trained to perform automatically. The result is the breakdown of the kinetic chain, loss of fluidity, and the emergence of [Antagonistic Tension](antagonistic-tension.md) and [Petit Bras](../technique/petit-bras.md).

Choking is not a character flaw — it is a predictable mechanical consequence of conscious interference.

---

## The Mechanism

Normal stroke execution runs automatically through Self 2's motor programs — developed through thousands of repetitions. The process operates far faster than conscious thought.

When Self 1 intrudes (due to pressure, fear, or critical thinking), it sends conflicting motor commands into a process that requires seamless sequencing. The results are predictable:

1. Opposing muscles co-contract instead of firing in sequence
2. [Antagonistic Tension](antagonistic-tension.md) develops across the kinetic chain
3. The arm "short-circuits" the body, producing [Petit Bras](../technique/petit-bras.md)
4. Racket-head speed collapses
5. The shot lacks power and precision — exactly what Self 1 was trying to prevent

## The "Choke vs. Flow" Distinction

The source material frames the difference between choking and the [Flow State](flow-state.md) as a single variable: **the source of effort**.

- **Choke**: effort comes from Self 1 forcing the outcome — strain, over-steering, muscular bracing
- **Flow**: effort comes from Self 2 running the program — lightness, rhythm, elastic release

This is the [Letting It Happen](../technique/letting-it-happen.md) vs. "Making It Happen" distinction in its most consequential form.

## Triggers

- High-pressure points (break points, set points, match points)
- [Fear of Failure](fear-of-failure.md) — the conscious equation of losing with personal worth
- Judgment spiral — a missed shot triggers self-criticism, which triggers anxiety, which triggers bracing
- "The Drift" — losing the [Between-Point Ritual](between-point-ritual.md) structure and allowing Self 1 to ruminate between points

## Prevention

Choking is prevented by everything that keeps Self 1 in its proper, non-interfering role:

| Tool | Function |
|---|---|
| [Between-Point Ritual](between-point-ritual.md) | Resets the system before each point |
| [Non-Judgmental Observation](non-judgmental-observation.md) | Prevents the judgment spiral that escalates to choking |
| [Bounce-Hit Technique](bounce-hit-technique.md) | Anchors Self 1 in present-moment sensory data |
| [Fear of Failure](fear-of-failure.md) reframe | Removes the threat perception that activates the amygdala |
| [Mushin](mushin.md) | Bypasses the amygdala's threat response at the neural level |
