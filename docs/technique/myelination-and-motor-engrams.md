# Myelination and Motor Engrams

In the manuals, elite execution depends on **myelinated motor engrams** rather than the loose idea of "muscle memory." Repetition builds neural pathways that fire faster, cleaner, and with less conscious interference.

## Core idea

- Clean repetition strengthens the route.
- Noise and hesitation slow the route.
- Fatigue can reduce control even when technique was once stable.

## Why it matters

The player has a small time window to read, decide, and act. Faster neural routing makes automatic execution possible before conscious thought can interfere.
