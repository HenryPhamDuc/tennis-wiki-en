# Deliberate Practice and Constraints

The manuals emphasize learning through targeted constraint, feedback, and specific failure rather than endless random repetition. The player is pushed just beyond comfort so the system has to adapt.

## Core idea

- Practice must expose a real weakness.
- Constraints shape the solution.
- Improvement comes from correction, not mindless volume.

## Why it matters

This is the training engine behind the whole vault. It explains how the body learns cleaner timing, how the mind reduces noise, and why elite habits come from structured difficulty.
