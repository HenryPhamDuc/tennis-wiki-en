## About

Tennis WIKI is an open encyclopedia compiled from the personal research system of **Henry Phạm** — tennis coach and sports biomechanics researcher based in Vietnam.

Contents include:

- **813+ articles** in English, compiled from personal notes, handbooks, and research material
- **Technique, biomechanics, tactics, psychology, fitness** — all major aspects
- **In-depth analyses** of the world's top players
- **Unique frameworks**: TFL 3.0 (Rhythm-Position-Movement), Dynamic Energy Transfer, Wave Theory, Tennis Control System
- **East Asian philosophy integration**: Tai Chi, Dantian, Mingmen, Chan Si Gong (Silk Reeling)
