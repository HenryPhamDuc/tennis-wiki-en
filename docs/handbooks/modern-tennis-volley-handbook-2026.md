## Core Concepts (Overview)

This handbook centers on the **"Still-Wall"** philosophy — replacing the traditional volley swing with the concept of a "static wall", using the opponent's pace rather than generating your own:

- **Redirection vs Generation:** Use the opponent's pace as the energy source, not a long swing
- **Pre-Impact Calibration:** Configure all parameters before contact
- **Universal Interface:** Standardized Continental grip for every volley situation
- **Net Dominance:** Footwork engine for net positioning
- **Physics of Contact:** Force vectors, impulse, momentum at the contact point
- **Final Execution:** Overhead/Smash — the point-deciding shot
